## Quote sources

All quotes are public-domain translations from established editions:

- Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations* — primarily George Long's 1862 translation
- Epictetus, *Discourses* and *Enchiridion* — Elizabeth Carter's 1758 translation and George Long's 1890 translation
- Seneca, *Letters from a Stoic* and *On the Shortness of Life* — Richard M. Gummere's Loeb editions (1917–1925), now in the public domain
- A small number from Diogenes, Pythagoras, Hesiod, Jerome — public-domain translations as cited in standard reference editions

If you spot a misattribution, please open an issue — accuracy matters more than convenience.
